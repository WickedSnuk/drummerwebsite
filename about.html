What is Practico ?

Practico is a focused, distraction-free practice space designed specifically for drummers. Whether you're working on rudiments, technique, timing, endurance, or full practice routines, Practico brings everything you need into one clean and organized place.

Most drummers get their exercises through scattered YouTube links, notes, and screenshots. Practico turns that chaos into structure. Here, you can watch your lesson videos, follow your exercises, organize your routines, and track your progress — all from one central hub.

Practico isn't a social platform. It's a practice tool.
The goal is simple: help drummers practice smarter, stay consistent, and grow faster.

What You Can Do in Practico

  • Play your lesson videos with a clean, distraction-free interface
  • Organize exercises by category (rudiments, grooves, fills, warm-ups, etc.)
  • Build your personal practice routine
  • Track your progress and consistency
  • Use built-in practice tools (metronome, timers, lists, etc.)

Why Practico Exists

As drummers, we collect practice material from many places — lesson videos, online exercises, rudiments, groove ideas, warmups, PDFs, and random inspiration clips. Over time, everything ends up spread across different apps, files, and links.

Practico brings all of that into one clean, organized practice space.
So instead of searching for what to work on, you can stay focused and keep growing.

The Vision

To create the simplest, cleanest, and most effective practice environment for drummers of all levels. Rookies, intermediates, advanced players — everyone needs structure. Practico gives you that structure without being overwhelming.
